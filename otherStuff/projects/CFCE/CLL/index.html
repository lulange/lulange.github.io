CFCE: CLL algorithms

CLL Stats:

The average number of turns for the algs given here is approximately 10.62.
10.62 plus the 75% chance of a U face turn (before the algorithm) gives,
assuming all the cases have an equal chance of occuring and ignoring skips, 11.37 turns for solving the corners.
