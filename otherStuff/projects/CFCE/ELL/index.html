CFCE: ELL algorithms

ELL Stats:

The average number of turns for the algs given here is ~10.83.
Plus the 1.5 for the AUFs before and after the ELL alg, this LL method averages ~23.4 speed optimized (with no rotations).
Note that some of these algs may leave off the end U/U'/U2.

ELL:

ELL has 29 cases separated by the what edges are flipped into 4 groups.

  • EPLL cases (no flips)
  • Adjacent two flip cases
  • Opposite two flip cases
  • Four flip cases
