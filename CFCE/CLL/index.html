CFCE: CLL algorithms

CLL Stats:

The average number of turns for the algs given here is approximately 10.43.
10.43 plus the 75% chance of a U face turn (before the algorithm) gives,
assuming all the cases have an equal chance of occuring, 11.18 turns for solving the corners.

Factoring in the chance of a case occuring and including a skip chance,
The average number of turns is 10.31 plus the 0.75 for U face AUF, adjust U Face.
In the end, you get an average of 11.06 turns for completely solving the corners.
