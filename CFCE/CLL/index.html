The CFCE method: CLL algorithms

CLL:

CLL has 42 cases separated by the corner pattern into 8 groups.

U cases:

The U cases have an average of 10 1/6 turns

UU:

R' U' R U' R' U2 R2 U R' U R U2 R'

UD:

F R U R' U' F'

UR:

U2 R2 D R' U2 R D' R' U2 R'

UL:

R2 D' R U2 R' D R U2 R'

UF:

R' U R U2 R' U' F' U F U R

UB:

U R' U L U' R U2 F U' F' L'

H cases:

The H cases have an average of 12 1/4 turns

HU:

R U2 R' U' R U R' U' R U' R'

HD:

F (R U R' U')3 F'

HR:

U F R U R' U' R U' R' U R U R' F'

HF:

r U' r' F U2 r2 F r U' r

S cases:

The S cases have an average of 8 5/6 turns

SU:

R U R' U R U2 R'

SD:

U2 R U R' U R' F R F' R U2 R'

SR:

F R' F' R U2 R U2 R'

SL:

R U' L' U R' U' L

SF:

U' F R' U2 R F' R' F U2 F' R

SB:

r' F2 r U2 r U' r' F
